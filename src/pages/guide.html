Welcome to YouTube Channel Notifier

YouTube Channel Notifier (YCN) is an intelligent Chrome extension that automatically tracks your YouTube viewing habits and sends you notifications when your favorite channels upload new videos. Unlike YouTube's noisy notification system, YCN only notifies you about channels you actually watch.

Pro Tip

YCN learns your viewing patterns automatically. You don't need to manually subscribe or configure anything - just watch YouTube normally!

How YCN Works

Smart Tracking

YCN watches your YouTube habits in the background, noting which channels you watch and how much of each video you complete.

Intelligent Filtering

Only channels you've watched 10+ videos from (with 50% completion) become eligible for notifications.

Smart Notifications

Get notified about new uploads from channels you genuinely care about, not every channel you've ever clicked on.

Relationship Scoring

YCN calculates your relationship strength with each channel based on frequency, recency, and engagement patterns.

Getting Started - Step by Step

1

Install & Pin the Extension

After installing YCN from the Chrome Web Store, pin it to your toolbar for easy access. Click the puzzle piece icon in your Chrome toolbar and pin YouTube Channel Notifier.

Why Pin?

Pinning gives you quick access to your channel statistics and current tracking status.

2

Watch YouTube Normally

Just browse and watch YouTube as you usually do! YCN will automatically start tracking channels you watch frequently. You'll see a small indicator in the extension popup showing which video is currently being tracked.

Important

YCN only counts videos you watch at least 50% of the way through without skipping. This ensures accurate tracking of your real interests.

3

Monitor Your Progress

Click the YCN icon to see your current tracking status. The popup shows:

  • Current Video: What you're watching now
  • Channels Tracked: Total channels being monitored
  • Ready for Approval: Channels with 10+ videos watched
  • Active Notifications: Approved channels sending notifications
4

Open Your Dashboard

Click "Open Dashboard" in the popup to access your full channel management interface. This is where the magic happens!

Common Questions & Troubleshooting

‚ùì Why aren't videos being tracked?

YCN only tracks videos you watch at least 50% through without skipping. Make sure you're actually watching, not just having videos open in background tabs.

‚ùì Why don't I see notifications yet?

You need to watch 10+ videos from a channel first, then manually approve it in your dashboard. YCN doesn't automatically enable notifications.

‚ùì Can I track music/long videos?

Yes! YCN tracks videos in the background even when you switch tabs or minimize Chrome. Perfect for music, podcasts, or long tutorials.

‚ùì How do I backup my data?

Use the Export Data feature in Settings. You can export to JSON (complete data), CSV (spreadsheet), OPML (RSS readers), or plain URLs.

‚ùì Is my data private?

Absolutely! All data is stored locally in your browser. With Ghost Protocol, even email notifications don't require storing your email address.
